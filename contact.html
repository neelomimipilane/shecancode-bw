Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join SheCanCode BW programs?

Our programs are open to girls and young women aged 12-25 across Botswana. Some advanced programs may have specific age or prerequisite requirements.

Are your programs free?

Yes, all our core programs are completely free for participants. We believe in removing financial barriers to tech education.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is needed for our beginner programs. We offer programs for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced learners.

How can my organization partner with you?

We welcome partnerships with corporations, educational institutions, and community organizations. Please contact us to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
