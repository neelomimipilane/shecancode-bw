About SheCanCode BW

Learn about our mission, vision, and the story behind our initiative to empower girls in technology across Botswana.

Our Story

SheCanCode BW was founded in 2025 by Neelo Mimi Pilane, a Computer Systems Engineering student at BAC. The initiative was created to empower young women in Botswana to explore and excel in technology, closing the gender gap in STEM fields.

SheCanCode BW provides hands-on learning opportunities, mentorship, and exposure to real-world tech experiences. From a student-led initiative, it is now a growing movement inspiring girls to pursue STEM careers and gain confidence in technology.

Our Vision

A future where women are equally represented and leading in technology across Botswana and Africa.

Our Mission

To equip girls and young women with skills, confidence, and inspiration to thrive in technology and innovation.

Our Approach

Inclusive Learning

Creating safe, supportive environments where girls can learn without barriers.

Community Building

Connecting girls with mentors and peers in the tech industry.

Sustainable Impact

Focusing on long-term career development and continuous learning.
