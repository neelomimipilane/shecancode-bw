Our Mission & Vision

To bridge the gender gap in technology by equipping girls and young women with technical skills, confidence, and mentorship to thrive in technology and innovation.

We envision a future where women are equally represented and leading in technology across Botswana and Africa, driving innovation and creating solutions for our communities.
